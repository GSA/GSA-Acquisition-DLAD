SUBPART 50.1 – EXTRAORDINARY CONTRACTUAL ACTIONS

(Revised October 24, 2016 through PROCLTR 2016-10)

50.101 General.

50.101-3 Records.

Records shall be maintained by the contracting officer in Records Management.

50.103 Contract adjustments.

50.103-5 Processing cases.

(S-90) The procuring organization responsible for processing a contractor's request is responsible for processing cases in accordance with DFARS PGI 250.103-5 and, following legal review by Office of Counsel, will send the case with recommendation for disposition through the HCA to the DLA Acquisition Operations Division for processing to the Agency Director for decision. The procuring organization is also responsible for establishing liaison and joint action with other Military Departments and other departments and agencies of the Government, as appropriate.

50.103-6 Disposition.

In accordance with DFARS 250.103-6, the DLA Acquisition Operations Division will maintain records of disposition in accordance with DFARS PGI 250.103-6.
