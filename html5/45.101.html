45.101 Definitions.

“Property Administrator” See 1.670.

“Stock Provided to Vendor (SPTV)” is SAP terminology that identifies and tracks items or components issued to DLA Distribution or a contractor for assembly or modification to make an end-item. Examples include items or components required for kits, uniforms, MREs, and government furnished material.

“Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI)” is used to characterize various programs in which Government-owned property is managed by the contractor.
