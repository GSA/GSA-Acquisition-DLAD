Subpart 6.2 - FULL AND OPEN COMPETITION AFTER EXCLUSION OF SOURCES

(Revised October 24, 2016 through PROCLTR 2016-10)

Parent topic: Part 6 - COMPETITION REQUIREMENTS

6.202 Establishing or maintaining alternative sources.

(b)(1) By memorandum dated July 30, 2015, the DLA Acquisition Director has delegated approval and signature authority to the HCAs for the determination and findings required to exclude a source under FAR 6.202(b)(1) for procurements within their contracting activity. This authority may not be further delegated.
