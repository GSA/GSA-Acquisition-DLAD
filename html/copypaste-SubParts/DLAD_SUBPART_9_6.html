SUBPART 9.6 – CONTRACTOR TEAM ARRANGEMENTS

(Revised August 30, 2019 through PROCLTR 2022-09)

Parent topic: PART 9 – CONTRACTOR QUALIFICATIONS

9.603 Policy.

(S-90) The contracting officer shall include procurement notes L34 Contractor Team Arrangements – Solicitation and Award and L35 Contractor Team Arrangements – Task and Delivery Orders in solicitations and awards, as applicable, when the use of Contractor Team Arrangements is contemplated (see section 4.190 and FAR subpart 9.6).
