SUBPART 4.76 —SUPPLIER PERFORMANCE RISK SYSTEM

(Revised September 6, 2024 through PROCLTR 2024-09)

Parent topic: PART 4 – ADMINISTRATIVE MATTERS

4.7602 Applicability.

In addition to the exclusions stated in DODI 5000.79 for the procurement of certain supplies or services, the use of SPRS risk assessments is not required for solicitations equal to or less than the micro purchase threshold.

4.7604 Solicitation provision.

The provision at DFARS 252.204-7024, Notice on the Use of the Supplier Performance Risk System, is not required in solicitations equal to or less than the micro purchase threshold.
