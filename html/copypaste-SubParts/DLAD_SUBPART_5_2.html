5.202 Exceptions.

(a)(13) Proposed contract actions exceeding $25,000 but not expected to exceed the SAT posted on DIBBS meet this exception when the solicitations contain:

(i) NSNs/Materials that are numeric or begin with letters “G,” “M,” “S,” or “L.”

(ii) A PIIN/PIID with the 9th position equal to “Q,” “T,” or “U.”

(iii) Delivery terms expressed in a number of days after date of award (ADO) for all proposed contract lines.

(iv) Incoterms that are the same for all proposed contract lines.
