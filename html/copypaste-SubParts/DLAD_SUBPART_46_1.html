Subpart 46.1 - GENERAL

(Revised November 9, 2016 through PROCLTR 2017-02)

Parent topic: Part 46 - QUALITY ASSURANCE

46.103 Contracting office responsibilities.

(a) Requirements are provided on the purchase request in EBS.

46.105 Contractor responsibilities.

(b) The contractor is required to maintain calibrated measuring and test equipment used for test and verification of products offered. The product specialist shall insert the TQ STO RT001 Measuring and Test Equipment in the PID.
