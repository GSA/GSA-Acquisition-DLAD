9.304 Exceptions.

FAT will not be applied for products identified to the following programs or assigned Acquisition Method Suffix Codes (AMSC):

(a) Reverse Engineering projects.

(b) Qualification with an AMSC Code T.

(c) Commercially available AMSC Code Z.

(d) Lack technical data AMSC Codes D, H, and P.
