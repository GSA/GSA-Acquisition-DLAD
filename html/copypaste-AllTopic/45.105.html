45.105 Contractors’ Property Management System Compliance.

(a) Conduct an analysis of the contractor’s property management policies, procedures, practices, and systems, as frequently as conditions warrant, in accordance with DoDI 4161.02, Accountability and Management of Government Contract Property, dated April 27, 2012 (https://www.esd.whs.mil/Portals/54/Documents/DD/issuances/dodi/416102p.pdf); and the

DoD Guidebook for Contract Property Administration, dated December 2014 (https://www.dau.edu/guidebooks/Shared%20Documents%20HTML/Guidebook%20for%20Contract%20Property%20Administration.aspx).
