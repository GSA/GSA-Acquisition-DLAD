17.9503 Pricing.

(a) A PV contract or other tailored logistics support contract must comply with one of the established PV pricing models in Subpart SUBPART 15.4.

(b) Catalog pricing. The initial catalog of DLA approved items available for ordering under the TLSC is created at time of contract award.
