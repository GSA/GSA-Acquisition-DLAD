16.503 Requirements contracts.

(a)(S-90) Guaranteed minimum (GM) purchase requirements and obligations. Contracting officers are not required to establish a GM for requirements contracts; however, if the contracting officer elects to include a GM in the solicitation and the award, the policies at 16.504(a)(1)(S-90 and 17.207(S-90) apply.
