4.9000 Policy.

Contracting officers shall include procurement note H17, Restriction on FSC 5962, Electronic Microcircuits, in solicitations and contracts equal to or under the micro-purchase threshold. Procurement note H17 applies only to procurements involving Federal Supply Class (FSC) code 5962, Electronic Microcircuits.
