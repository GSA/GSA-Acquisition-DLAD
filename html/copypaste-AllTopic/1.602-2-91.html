1.602-2-91 Pricing Oversight Program.

(a) The Pricing Oversight Program implements consistent, process-driven verification and/or statistical sampling validation to ensure procuring organizations use acceptable cost or pricing analysis techniques. It promotes proven methodologies in acquisition strategy planning, evaluation, and award.

(b) For procedures associated with the Pricing Oversight Program, see All DLA Official Issuances ( https://issue-p.dla.mil/Pages/ViewAllIssuances.aspx ). Select “Issuance #”, and scroll to DLAM 5025.08, Pricing Oversight Program.
