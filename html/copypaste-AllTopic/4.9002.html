4.9002 Prohibition.

(a) The offeror shall not propose to deliver FSC 5962, Electronic Microcircuits, from a covered nation to the Government under any solicitation or resulting contract.

(b) If the contracting officer is unable to determine whether the FSC 5962, Electronic Microcircuits is from a covered nation based on traceability documentation or other means on the offeror’s representation, then the award shall not be made to that offeror.
