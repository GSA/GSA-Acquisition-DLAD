(b) Market Research for Commercial Items and Commerciality Determination Memorandum for Record (MRCICDM).

The contracting officer shall use the MRCICDM format as prescribed in 13.501(b)(3). The contracting officer shall include a “Source Selection Information” legend when applicable pursuant to FAR 2.101 and 3.104. Procuring organizations may append the MRCICDM to the acquisition plan, and the acquisition plan may refer to the MRCICDM in sections regarding market research and sustainability. This Market Research for Commercial Items and Commerciality Determination Memorandum (MRCICDM) for Record formatis for illustration purposes only. It mirrors the fillable version in the contract writing system.
